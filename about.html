About

I'm a film photographer based in Charleston, South Carolina, documenting the Lowcountry through analog photography.

My work focuses on the intersection of light, architecture, and landscape that makes this region unique. I shoot primarily on 35mm and medium format film, drawn to the deliberate process and distinctive rendering that analog photography provides.

Charleston's historic architecture, coastal marshes, and Southern light offer endless subjects. Each photograph is an exercise in patience—waiting for the right moment, trusting the process, and embracing the imperfections that make film photography authentic.
